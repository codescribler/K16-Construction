How to Prepare for Your Consultation

To make the most of your consultation, consider preparing:

  • Ideas & Inspiration: Collect images or examples of styles you like
  • Budget Range: Have a rough idea of what you're comfortable investing
  • Timeline: Consider when you'd ideally like the work completed
  • Questions: Write down any specific concerns or requirements
  • Access: Ensure we can see all relevant areas of your property
Note: Don't worry if you're not sure about everything - we're here to guide you through the process!
