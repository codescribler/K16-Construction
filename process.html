Our Process

A transparent, professional approach from consultation to completion

Building Your Dream, Step by Step

At K16 Contracts, we believe in complete transparency. You'll know exactly what to expect at every stage of your project.

Our proven process has been refined over 25 years to ensure minimal disruption to your daily life while delivering exceptional results on time and on budget.

Clear Communication: Regular updates throughout your project
Fixed Timeline: We stick to agreed schedules
Quality Control: Regular inspections ensure high standards

What Makes Our Process Different?

  • No hidden costs or surprise charges
  • Dedicated project manager for your build
  • Clean and tidy work site maintained daily
  • Flexible payment options available
  • Full documentation and warranties provided

Your Journey With K16 Contracts

1

Initial Consultation

Free, no-obligation home assessment

We begin by visiting your home to understand your needs, assess the project scope, and discuss your vision. This consultation is completely free with no obligation to proceed.

What happens during consultation:
  • Discussion of your requirements and budget
  • Site assessment and measurements
  • Initial design ideas and suggestions
  • Timeline expectations
  • Answer any questions you have
Duration: 1-2 hours

What to prepare:

  • Any plans or drawings you have
  • Budget range in mind
  • Timeline preferences
  • Style preferences or examples
Book Your Free Consultation
2

Detailed Quote & Planning

Comprehensive, transparent pricing

Following the consultation, we prepare a detailed quote that breaks down all costs, timelines, and project specifications. No hidden extras, no surprises.

Your quote includes:
  • Itemized cost breakdown
  • Materials specifications
  • Project timeline with key milestones
  • Payment schedule options
  • Terms and conditions
Delivery: Within 3-5 days

What we provide:

  • Written quote valid for 30 days
  • Detailed project scope
  • 3D visualizations (where applicable)
  • References available on request
No pressure - take your time to review and compare
3

Project Execution

Quality construction with minimal disruption

Once approved, our experienced team begins work on your project. We maintain a clean, safe work environment and keep disruption to your daily routine to an absolute minimum.

During construction:
  • Daily site cleanup and organization
  • Regular progress updates
  • Dedicated project manager on-site
  • Quality checks at each stage
  • Flexibility for minor adjustments
Our Commitments
  • Punctuality: Team arrives on time
  • Cleanliness: Daily site cleanup
  • Communication: Weekly progress meetings
  • Safety: Full insurance and compliance
  • Quality: Only skilled tradespeople
4

Completion & Aftercare

Your satisfaction is our priority

Upon completion, we conduct a thorough walkthrough with you to ensure everything meets your expectations. We remain available for any snagging or questions after the project is finished.

Post-completion includes:
  • Detailed project walkthrough
  • Snagging list resolution
  • All documentation and certificates
  • Warranty information
  • Ongoing support and advice
Our Guarantees
  • 10-year structural guarantee
  • 2-year workmanship warranty
  • 30-day snagging period
  • Lifetime customer support
We're still here long after the job is done
