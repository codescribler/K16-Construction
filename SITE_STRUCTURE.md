# K16 Contracts - Website Structure Plan

## Static Site Technology Stack
- **HTML5** - Semantic markup
- **CSS Framework** - Bootstrap 5 (responsive, well-documented, component-rich)
- **JavaScript** - Vanilla JS for interactions (form validation, image galleries, smooth scrolling)
- **Build Tools** - None initially (pure static), can add later if needed

## Page Structure

### 1. Homepage (index.html)
**Purpose**: Convert visitors by addressing their main pain points immediately
**Key Sections**:
- Hero section: "Building Quality Homes for Growing Families Since 1998"
- Problem statement: Address overcrowding and unreliable builders
- Services overview (Extensions, Renovations, Full Builds)
- Why Choose K16: Family business, 25+ years experience, on-time delivery
- Recent projects gallery (6-8 images)
- Testimonials carousel
- Clear CTA: "Book Your Free Consultation"

### 2. Services (services.html)
**Purpose**: Detail specific offerings with clear benefits
**Sections**:
- Home Extensions
  - Extra bedrooms for growing families
  - Kitchen extensions
  - Living space expansions
- Renovations & Refurbishments
  - Kitchen makeovers
  - Bathroom installations
  - Bedroom redesigns
- Full Home Builds
- Each service includes: process, timeline, benefits, example images

### 3. Our Process (process.html)
**Purpose**: Build trust through transparency
**Content**:
1. Initial Consultation (Free home assessment)
2. Detailed Quote (Fully costed with timelines)
3. Project Execution (Quality work with minimal disruption)
4. Post-Completion Support (Responsive aftercare)
- Visual timeline/steps
- What to expect at each stage
- Communication promise

### 4. Portfolio/Projects (portfolio.html)
**Purpose**: Showcase quality and build confidence
**Features**:
- Filter by project type (Extensions, Kitchens, Bathrooms, etc.)
- Before/after comparisons
- Project details (timeline, scope, challenges solved)
- Client testimonials for each project
- Lightbox gallery for detailed viewing

### 5. About Us (about.html)
**Purpose**: Build trust through personal connection
**Content**:
- Family business story (Since 1998)
- Father-son tradition narrative
- Our values: Quality, Reliability, Family Reputation
- Team introduction
- Certifications and memberships
- Why we're different from cowboys builders

### 6. Why Choose K16 (why-us.html)
**Purpose**: Differentiation from competitors
**Key Points**:
- 25+ years of experience
- Family reputation at stake
- Proven track record vs cowboy builders
- Clean & tidy work practices
- On-time delivery promise
- Transparent pricing
- Extensive portfolio of completed projects

### 7. Testimonials (testimonials.html)
**Purpose**: Social proof
**Content**:
- Client success stories
- Video testimonials (if available)
- Written reviews with photos
- Project outcomes
- Focus on: timeliness, cleanliness, quality, communication

### 8. Contact (contact.html)
**Purpose**: Generate leads
**Features**:
- Contact form with project type selection
- Phone number prominently displayed
- Email address
- Service area map
- Office hours
- Free consultation booking widget
- FAQ section addressing common concerns

### 9. Free Consultation (consultation.html)
**Purpose**: Dedicated conversion page
**Content**:
- What's included in free consultation
- No-obligation assessment
- What to prepare
- Booking form
- Testimonials about consultation experience

## Common Components (Across All Pages)

### Header
- Logo
- Main navigation
- Phone number (clickable on mobile)
- "Book Consultation" CTA button

### Footer
- Contact information
- Quick links
- Service areas
- Certifications/memberships
- Social media links
- Copyright

## Design Considerations
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Fast loading (optimize images)
- Accessibility compliant
- Clear CTAs on every page
- Trust signals throughout (years in business, family owned, testimonials)
- Professional but approachable tone

## SEO Optimization
- Local SEO focus (location-based keywords)
- Structured data for local business
- Meta descriptions addressing pain points
- Alt text for all images
- Fast page load speeds
